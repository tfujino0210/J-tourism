While Japan was a major source of outbound tourists in the world for a long time,
but Japan was one of major destinations of overseas travelers in 2010s too.
The growth in the number of inbound visitors in 2010s was remarkable.
Actually, the number of inbound travelers in 2019 was 32 millions,
which was four times of that in 2010. 2011 earthquake and tsunami in Japan
was overcome and we hope to get over the pandemic too.

Tourists from East Asia greatly contributed to the Japanese tourism in 2010s.
The number of visitors from China grew rapidly and they accounted for
the largest portion in 2019.

Source: Japan National Tourism Organization (Trends by Country/Area)

The sum of not only travelers from East Asia, but also those from other areas
showed significant increase in 2010s.
The number of those from many countries were doubled or more in 2010s.
For example, the number of tourists from Thaniland rose from 0.17 million
in 2010 to 1.25 million in 2019.
